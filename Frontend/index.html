About RUET


    Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET) is the second oldest engineering university in Bangladesh. It was founded in 1964 as a faculty of Engineering under the University of Rajshahi providing four years Bachelor degree in Civil, Electrical and Electronic and Mechanical Engineering. In 1986, it was converted to an autonomous institution called Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology.
